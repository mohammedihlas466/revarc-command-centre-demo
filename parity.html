How RevArc handles rate gaps
Angle = booking channel · distance from centre = size of the rate gap · dot size = revenue exposure. The sweep re-scans every channel continuously. Since the EU's 2024 Digital Markets Act ended rate-parity clauses, RevArc never forces a rate match: drift in your own rates within the ±6% guardrail is auto-realigned through the channel manager, while larger corrections and every OTA-funded undercut are held for human sign-off — the play is to win the direct booking, not to chase an OTA down.
